### Phase 1 — Foundations

- [ ] **[Calculus I (SMA0301)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0301&codcur=55030&codhab=100)**
  - **Book**: *Um curso de cálculo, Volume 1* — Guidorizzi
    **Topics Covered**: Limits, continuity, derivatives and applications, single-variable integrals and applications. Foundation for any continuous formalism used in quantum mechanics.

- [ ] **[Linear Algebra (SMA0375)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0375&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Book**: *Linear Algebra Done Right* — Sheldon Axler
    **Topics Covered**: Vector spaces, linear maps, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, inner product spaces, operators on finite-dimensional spaces, spectral theorem, normal/self-adjoint/unitary operators, singular value decomposition. **The absolute core of quantum computing** — qubits are vectors and quantum gates are unitary operators.

### Phase 2 — Multivariable Calculus and Complex Analysis

- [ ] **[Calculus II (SMA0332)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0332&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Book**: *Um curso de cálculo, Volume 2* — Guidorizzi
    **Topics Covered**: Numerical sequences and series, power series, Taylor and Fourier series. Essential for expansions used in perturbation theory and approximation methods.

- [ ] **Calculus III**
  - **Book**: *Um curso de cálculo, Volume 3* — Guidorizzi
    **Topics Covered**: Functions of several variables, partial derivatives, gradient, multiple integrals, line and surface integrals, Green's, Stokes', and Gauss' theorems. Needed for formulating operators and wavefunctions in multiple dimensions.

- [ ] **[Complex Variable Functions (SME0245)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SME0245&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Complex numbers, analytic functions, Cauchy-Riemann equations, complex integration, Cauchy's theorem, Laurent series, residues. Quantum amplitudes live in ℂ — mandatory territory.

### Phase 3 — Differential Equations and Mechanics

- [ ] **Calculus IV / [Ordinary Differential Equations (SME0240)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SME0240&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Book**: *Um curso de cálculo, Volume 4* — Guidorizzi
    **Topics Covered**: First- and second-order ODEs, linear systems, series solutions, Laplace transforms. The Schrödinger equation is a PDE, but reduces to ODEs in separable problems.

- [ ] **[Physics I (7600005)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=7600005&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Book**: *Curso de Física Básica, Vol. 1 — Mecânica* — Moisés Nussenzveig
    **Topics Covered**: Kinematics, Newton's laws, work and energy, linear and angular momentum, rigid bodies, harmonic oscillator, gravitation. The harmonic oscillator is the central example that reappears throughout quantum mechanics.

### Phase 4 — Waves, Thermodynamics, and Electromagnetism

- [ ] **[Physics II (7600006)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=7600006&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Book**: *Curso de Física Básica, Vol. 2 — Fluidos, Oscilações e Ondas, Calor* — Moisés Nussenzveig
    **Topics Covered**: Forced and damped oscillations, mechanical waves, superposition, interference, introductory thermodynamics and statistical mechanics. All wave intuition used in QM comes from here.

- [ ] **Physics III — Electromagnetism**
  - **Book**: *Curso de Física Básica, Vol. 3 — Eletromagnetismo* — Moisés Nussenzveig
    **Topics Covered**: Electrostatics, Gauss's law, magnetostatics, Ampère's law, induction, Maxwell's equations, electromagnetic waves. Photons, light-matter interaction, and superconducting qubits all start here.

- [ ] **[Partial Differential Equations (SMA0169)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0169&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Heat, wave, and Laplace equations, separation of variables, Fourier series, boundary value problems, Sturm-Liouville problems. The Schrödinger equation is a PDE — separation of variables is the standard technique for solving it.

### Phase 5 — Modern Physics and Probability

- [ ] **Physics IV — Optics, Relativity, and Quantum Physics**
  - **Book**: *Curso de Física Básica, Vol. 4 — Ótica, Relatividade, Física Quântica* — Moisés Nussenzveig
    **Topics Covered**: Wave optics, polarization, special relativity, blackbody radiation, photoelectric effect, Bohr model, wave-particle duality, uncertainty principle, introduction to the Schrödinger equation. **First serious contact with quantum physics.**

- [ ] **[Introduction to Measure Theory and Probability (SMA0389)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0389&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Probability spaces, random variables, distributions, expectation and variance, law of large numbers, central limit theorem. The interpretation of QM is statistical — measurements are samples from distributions.

### Phase 6 — Analytical Mechanics and Functional Analysis

- [ ] **Classical Mechanics (Lagrangian and Hamiltonian)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Variational principle, Euler-Lagrange equations, Hamiltonian formalism, canonical transformations, Poisson brackets, symmetries and conservation laws (Noether). The Hamiltonian formalism is the direct bridge to quantization.

- [ ] **[Introduction to Functional Analysis (SMA0120)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0120&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Banach and Hilbert spaces, bounded linear operators, compact operators, self-adjoint operators, spectral theorem in infinite dimensions, L² spaces. Quantum states live in Hilbert spaces — this is the "grown-up" version of linear algebra.

### Phase 7 — Quantum Mechanics

- [ ] **[Quantum Mechanics for Mathematicians (SMA0379)](https://uspdigital.usp.br/jupiterweb/obterDisciplina?sgldis=SMA0379&codcur=55030&codhab=200)**
  - **Topics Covered**: Postulates of quantum mechanics, Schrödinger equation, infinite well, harmonic oscillator, angular momentum, hydrogen atom, spin-1/2, two-level systems, perturbation theory, entanglement, Bell inequalities, measurement postulates (von Neumann), density operator.

### Phase 8 — Quantum Computing

- [ ] **Quantum Computation and Quantum Information**
  - **Book**: *Quantum Computation and Quantum Information* — Nielsen & Chuang ("Mike & Ike")
    **Topics Covered**:
    - **Part I — Foundations**: qubits, Bloch sphere, quantum gates (X, Y, Z, H, S, T, CNOT), quantum circuits, no-cloning theorem, quantum teleportation, superdense coding.
    - **Part II — Algorithms**: Deutsch-Jozsa, Bernstein-Vazirani, Simon's algorithm, quantum Fourier transform, phase estimation, Shor's algorithm (factoring), Grover's algorithm (search), quantum simulation.
    - **Part III — Quantum Information**: density operator, Schmidt decomposition, purification, quantum channels, quantum operations (Kraus), von Neumann entropy, quantum mutual information.
    - **Part IV — Error Correction**: 3-qubit code, Shor code, stabilizer formalism, CSS codes, surface code, threshold theorem, fault-tolerant quantum computation.
